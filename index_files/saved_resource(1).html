Strategic Vision & Development Axes

Our integrated vision is rooted in agronomy, aiming to synchronize Egyptian production with European needs through transparent, value-creating chains.

1

Exclusive Variety Licenses

We partner with seed providers to grow protected/niche varieties tailored to high-demand EU buyers.

  • Identify client-specific needs
  • Secure licenses from breeders
  • Cultivate under strict protocols
  • Offer reliability, transparency, and full control
2

Own Farms & Contract Farming

We operate our own land and collaborate via contract farming to control the full chain:

  • Total traceability
  • Social & environmental compliance
  • Agronomic/logistics optimization
  • Secure volumes for buyers
3

IQF Processing

Fresh produce not fit for export is transformed via IQF into frozen lines. We're building IQF capacity to reduce waste and partner with EU frozen distributors (retail, co-packing, health/snacking).

4

Nursery & Seedling Exports

In response to reduced production capacity in Southern Europe, we grow and export young plants (20–30 cm) in collaboration with breeders:

  • Offset local production gaps
  • Offer reliable early-stage seedlings

We grow more than fruit—we cultivate the future of agriculture.
